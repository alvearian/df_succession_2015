language_FINNISH

[OBJECT:LANGUAGE]
-- Finnish language for Dwarf Fortress .31.12 by TomiTapio (Twitter: @Tomi_Tapio). Without the important ä ö Ä Ö letters (rear vowels), using a o instead, for graphical tilesets.
--for Dwarf Fortress .31.12 in 2010-July, updated for .34 in 2012-March, updated for .42 in 2015-December

-- includes the rude words for OldGenesis mod.
-- aeiou/äeiöy are front/rear vowel groups.
-- Using imperative verb form for better sounding language. Tuo! Syö! Juo! Tee!
-- "-inen" is adjective postfix.  "-mus" is noun-maker.
-- all the two-syllable words are (or used to be) core words in Finnish.
-- verified with WinMerge to have all the words that language_DWARF has.
-- pronounciation guides: http://www.cs.tut.fi/~jkorpela/finnish.pronunciation.html , http://www.bbc.co.uk/dna/h2g2/A22548684 , http://www.phantomranch.net/folkdanc/alphabet/finnish.htm
